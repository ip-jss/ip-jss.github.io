Jessica Ip

I am a UBC researcher looking for full-time opportunities in Research/UX/Project Management starting in June 2020.

I recently completed my bachelor's degree in Cognitive Systems at the University of British Columbia. For the past four years, I’ve had the honour of collaborating with an eclectic group of talented researchers and developers at the UBC Visual Cognition Lab, in which I investigated the visual perception in graphical displays. During this time I was also a student researcher in a cross-institutional collaboration between the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems and the UBC Sensory Perception and Research Group on the Haptipedia project. My earlier research experiences were also in visualization on the WebValue Charts team in the Laboratory for Computational Intelligence.

Research

My research interests lie in the intersection of human-computer interaction, information visualization, and perceptual processing. I’m especially curious about the perceptual mechanisms that guide decision-making and how these processes inform the design of new information and visualization systems.
