Jessica Ip

I am a PhD candidate in Cognitive Science at Brown University studying the neurocognitive correlates of (multi)sensory perception and memory in healthy and pathological aging, advised by Dr. William Heindel and Dr. Elena Festa at the Aging and Cognition Lab. I am widely interested in translational and computational clinical neuroscience, multimodal sensory interfaces, age-related neurodegeneration, and analytical methods (AI/ML) for large-scale health data.
