Jessica Ip

I am a senior dual degree candidate in Computer Science and Statistics (B.Sc.) and Cognitive Systems (B.A.) at the University of British Columbia (UBC). My research interests lie in HCI, information visualization, perceptual processing, and human-centered technology.

I'm currently a Research Assistant at the UBC Visual Cognition Lab, where I investigate the perceptual properties of scatterplots with Prof. Ronald A. Rensink

Previously, I researched various incentives and methods ... with Prof. Karon E. MacLean and Dr. Hasti Seifi at the UBC Sensory Perception and Research Group.
